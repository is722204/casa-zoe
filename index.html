Welcome to Casa Zoe! Casa Zoe Care offers the care that life and care for the elderly requires, in an incredible location in Ajijic, Jalisco. Our community is located just 1 block from the lake, which allows us to provide our guests with recreational opportunities full of color and a magnificent climate. As Mexicans, it is natural to take care of our grandparents, to be kind and respectful. We venerate and care for seniors we want to protect them Discover a full and enriching life with Casa Zoé!

Mission

Our mission is to provide personalized services that are characterized by warmth, professionalism, and dedication, thereby fostering a sense of familiarity and enabling you to appreciate the distinctive atmosphere and enriching culture of Ajijic, Jalisco.



Vision

To provide the well-being that older individuals deserve through assisted living, with professional health care that ensures a comfortable and enjoyable stay. We strive to offer a quality experience that you will be proud to share with your loved ones.

ASSISTED LIVING

Assisted living is chosen for different reasons and we know no two stories are exactly alike. Some individuals choose assisted living because they need a little help living life fully. Some crave social opportunities and the close comfort of friends. Others no longer want the responsibility of upkeep for their home, and want to benefit from others to cook, clean, do laundry and more. No matter your reason for a move to assisted living, you can find what you need here at Sage Glendale.

Assisted Living Fees Include:

  • 24-hour trained on-site staffing
  • 3 meals daily, plus snacks
  • Recreational and physical activities
  • Cognitive-occupational dynamics
  • Medical follow-up

MEMORY CARE

We know that living with memory loss due to dementia is challenging. We look past the disease and focus on the individual. By providing customized care that’s exactly what someone needs to live safely and with dignity, and by getting to know residents personally, we can ensure that those in memory care at Sage Glendale will thrive while living life in our community. We’ll ensure the support they need, along with fun, life-enriching opportunities every day.

Memory care Fees Include:

  • 24-hour trained on-site staffing
  • 3 meals daily, plus snacks
  • Recreational and physical activities
  • Cognitive-occupational dynamics
  • Medical follow-up

HOME CARE

Our Home Care services is designed to provide specialized assistance to seniors who prefer to live in the comfort of their own homes. We tailor our care to their lifestyle, desires and needs, considering all the elements to enhance their quality of life.
