Discover a Full and Enriching Life

Casa Zoe offers the care that life for the elderly requires in an incredible location in Ajijic, Jalisco.

Our community is located just 1 block from the lake, which allows us to provide our guests with recreational opportunities full of color and a magnificent climate. As Mexicans, it is natural to take care of our grandparents, to be kind and respectful. We venerate and care for seniors. We want to protect them.

¡Discover a full and enriching life with Casa Zoé!

Our Care Services

Comprehensive care options designed to meet the unique needs of each individual

Assisted Living

Assisted living is chosen for different reasons and we know no two stories are exactly alike. Some individuals choose assisted living because they need a little help living life fully.

  • 24-hour trained on-site staffing
  • 3 meals daily, plus snacks
  • Recreational and physical activities
  • Medical follow-up

Memory Care

We know that living with memory loss due to dementia is challenging. We look past the disease and focus on the individual.

  • Specialized memory care staff
  • Structured daily routines
  • Cognitive stimulation activities
  • Secure environment

Home Care

Our Home Care services are designed to provide specialized assistance to seniors who prefer to live in the comfort of their own homes.

  • Nursing care
  • Personal caregivers
  • Housekeeping services
  • Companionship
