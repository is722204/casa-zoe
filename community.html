Welcome to Our Community

Thoughtfully designed for seniors seeking a healthy, active, and fulfilling lifestyle in the beautiful surroundings of Lake Chapala.

Our Philosophy

At Casa Zoe, we believe in creating a community that fosters independence while providing the support needed for a vibrant senior lifestyle. Our approach combines modern amenities with personalized care.

The Lake Chapala Advantage

Nestled by the shores of Mexico's largest freshwater lake, our community offers year-round perfect weather, stunning views, and a welcoming international community that makes every day feel like a vacation.
